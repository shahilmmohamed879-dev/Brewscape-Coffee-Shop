HOW TO
BREW
PERFECT
COFFEE

Whether you're a first-time brewer or a seasoned barista, our guide will help you extract every last note of flavour from your beans.

1
Grind Fresh
Grind only what you need right before brewing. Freshly ground coffee preserves aromatics that pre-ground loses within minutes.
2
Measure Precisely
Use a ratio of 1:15 to 1:17 (coffee to water by weight). A kitchen scale makes all the difference between good and great.
3
Temperature Matters
Water at 92–96°C extracts the sweet spot. Boiling scorches the grounds; too cool under-extracts and tastes sour.
4
Enjoy Immediately
Coffee is at its best in the first 20 minutes. Pour into a pre-warmed cup and savour the moment.
